About

Combining analytical rigor with civic dedication

My interest in technology began in December 2020, when a friend introduced me to game development on Roblox. That moment sparked years of hands-on experimentation in 3D modeling, Lua programming, and collaborative game development. Together with friends, I built projects we genuinely wanted to play or used as a way to explore new technical ideas. Over time, my focus shifted away from gaming itself toward creating work with broader real-world impact. After experimenting with C++ and app development, I found my direction in ML and Data Science. Since then, I have been working on machine learning and data-driven projects while deepening my understanding of history, economics, and the startup ecosystem through books and lectures, including works such as Zero to One. In 2025, I was elected to the Tallinn Youth Council, where I actively work on initiatives aimed at improving the quality of life for young people in Tallinn. This role allows me to combine my technical background with civic engagement, using data and technology to support better decision-making and meaningful youth-focused change.
