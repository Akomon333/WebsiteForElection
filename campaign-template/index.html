About Me

I want to improve the school system and support students' personal growth. I also enjoy programming in my free time.

I study at our local high school and have organized peer tutoring and coding clubs. I believe practical skills, mental-health support, and student-led activities make schools stronger.

Issues & Positions

Non-formal Education

Introduce workshops, community-led classes, and project-based learning that complement the curriculum and help students explore skills outside standard lessons.

Hobbies & Training

Encourage schools to build programs around students' hobbies — coding, arts, sports — and offer flexible scheduling for training and mentorship.

Hot Drinks in Breaks

Ensure safe access to hot beverages during breaks to support student well-being — supervised, affordable, and available at all break times.

Funding for Schools

Advocate for increased funding focused on learning materials, mental health services, library resources, and extracurricular programs.
